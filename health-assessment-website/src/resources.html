üë• When to Talk to Someone

You should reach out for help if you:

  • Feel sad or empty for more than 2 weeks
  • Have thoughts of hurting yourself or others
  • Can't concentrate at school or work
  • Have significant changes in sleep or eating
  • Feel overwhelmed by emotions
  • Use drugs or alcohol to cope
  • Feel isolated or withdrawn from others
  • Have experienced trauma or loss

Remember: Asking for help is a sign of strength, not weakness.
